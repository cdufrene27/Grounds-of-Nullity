Advocate's Reference Guide for Marriage Nullity Cases

How Can One Gain Freedom to Marry Again?

Options for Freedom to Remarry:

  • Dissolution of the Bond (Pauline Privilege, Favor of Faith, Non-consummation)
  • Declaration of Nullity (Based on defective consent, impediments, or defect of form)

Key Principle:

  • Catholics: Time of church ceremony determines validity
  • Non-Catholics: Entire duration of common life is considered for validity

Valid Marriage Consent Requires Three Elements

For a marriage to be valid, each spouse must be ABLE, KNOWING, and WILLING to marry:

🧠

ABLE

Psychological Capacity

Must have sufficient mental and emotional capacity to understand and fulfill marriage obligations

↓
🧐

KNOWING

Intellect

Must understand what marriage is and know relevant facts about the spouse

↓
💭

WILLING

Will

Must freely choose marriage without excluding essential elements or being coerced

↓

If any element is defective at the time of marriage, the marriage may be declared null.
